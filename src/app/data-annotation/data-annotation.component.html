Data Annotation &
Labeling















Image Annotation

This involves labeling objects, regions of interest, bounding boxes, or semantic segmentation masks within images. It can be used for object detection, image classification, or object tracking tasks.





Text Annotation

Text annotation involves labeling or categorizing text data, such as sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, or part-of-speech tagging.





Audio Annotation

In audio annotation, speech or sound segments are labeled with relevant information, such as speaker identification, transcription, or emotion recognition.





Video Annotation

Video annotation involves labeling objects, actions, or events within videos. It can be used for activity recognition, video surveillance, or autonomous driving applications.
